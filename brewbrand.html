Our Eco-Friendly Packaging

Our reusable coffee sleeves are designed with both style and sustainability in mind. Each sleeve is crafted from high-quality, eco-friendly materials that are built to last.

  • Sustainable – made from recyclable / biodegradable materials.
  • Reusable or compostable – doesn’t end up in landfills.
  • Safe & durable – protects the product without plastic.
  • Customizable designs for businesses
  • Fits most standard cup sizes

By using an Ecosip sleeve, you're helping to reduce single-use waste and make a positive impact on the environment. Each sleeve can replace hundreds of disposable alternatives.
